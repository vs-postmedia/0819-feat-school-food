SCHOOL MEAL FUNDING STARTS THIS YEAR

  • The provincial government has set aside $71.5 million per year for the next three years to fund meal programs for elementary and secondary students across B.C. Advocates say the program needs far more money to reach a truly “universal” scale.Scroll for more...

  • The Ministry has allocated about $120 per student in the densely populated Lower Mainland.

  • Additional funding is provided to districts based on a number of factors, including remote school locations and unique needs of the student body.

  • $350,000 in funding for the remote Stikine district works out to roughly $2,850 per student.
